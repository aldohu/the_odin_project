Quickie kajmak

Description

  • 1 cup feta cheese
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 2 cups cream cheese, softened
  1. Press feta cheese through a sieve until most of liquid has drained. Beat drained feta cheese, sour cream, and cream cheese in a bowl until smooth. Refrigerate until flavors blend, at least 30 minutes. Bring to room temperature before serving.
